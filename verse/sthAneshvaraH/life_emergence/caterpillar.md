title = "Caterpillar"
+++

When turned into butterfly,  
Grew just a single wing.  
Could neither crawl nor fly.  

The nestling,  
Flopped her wings,  
Peeped outside of the tiny nest,  
And snuggled back.

The gypsy,  
Gave him the ultimatum,  
That she would leave him,  
And kissed him deep.
